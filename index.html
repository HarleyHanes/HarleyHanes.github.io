Welcome!

Thank you for visiting my webpage. I am an applied mathematician specializing in reduced-order modeling and verification, validation, and uncertainty quantification (VVUQ). I have extensive experience with diverse application spaces including radiation transport, Navier-Stokes, chemical reactors, and ecological models of disease transmission.

I am currently a PhD candidate in Applied Mathematics at North Carolina State University (advisor Ralph C. Smith) graduating in May 2026 and searching for positions as a scientific software developer. I previously received my M.S. in Computational Science, B.S. in Mathematics, and B.S.P.H. in Public Health from Tulane University (advisor James (Mac) Hyman ).
